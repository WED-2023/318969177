    • About Me



    Pat Gelsinger is the former CEO of Intel. He has worked in technology for many years. He was born in Pennsylvania in 1961.




    Pat loved learning about computers. He finished college very early - by the age of 18.



    He later completed his studies at Santa Clara University and Stanford.



    He joined Intel in 1979 and helped develop USB and Wi-Fi.



    In 2001, he became Intels Chief Technology Officer. He later worked at EMC and VMware.





    Beyond his work, Pat is a Christian, and his faith is important to him- guides his leadership and personal values.



    He believes in hard work, balance, and helping others. Hes passionate about mentoring young professionals and shaping the next generation of tech leaders.



    One of his mentors was Andy Grove, Intels former CEO.



    In his free time, he enjoys family and giving back to the community.
